Common Questions

FAQs

Everything you need to know before your first visit. Still have questions? Call or message us directly.

Botox injections involve minimal discomfort — most clients describe a tiny pinch. We use ultra-fine needles and can apply topical numbing cream beforehand for added comfort.
Botox results typically last 4–6 months. Dermal fillers can last 9–18 months depending on the area. Skin treatments like microneedling provide cumulative benefits that continue improving with sessions.
Most treatments are non-invasive with zero to minimal downtime. Botox and fillers allow you to return to daily activities immediately. Some temporary redness or swelling can occur for a few hours.
Absolutely. All treatments are performed by certified medical professionals using FDA-approved products. We conduct a thorough consultation and medical review before any procedure to ensure your safety.
Arrive with a clean face, avoid blood-thinning medications (aspirin, ibuprofen) 3 days prior if possible, and avoid alcohol 24 hours before. We'll provide specific instructions based on your planned treatment.
